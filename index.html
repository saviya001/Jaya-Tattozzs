OUR STUDIO

Established in 2020, JAYA TATTOOZ has rapidly become one of Hettipola's most respected tattoo studios. Our talented artists masterfully blend traditional techniques with modern innovation to create truly unique pieces.

Your safety and comfort are paramount. We strictly adhere to the highest standards of hygiene and sterilization, using only premium quality, vegan-friendly inks and single-use needles for every client.
